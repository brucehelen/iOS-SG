Recommendation diet for high blood pressure

a) S - Salt Restriction. Daily intake of salt recommends should be lower than 6grams.

  • It could reduce systolic blood pressure around 2-8 millimeters of mercury (mmHg)

b) A - Alcohol limitation. Daily intake of alcohol is 355ml of beer, 148ml of wine or 44ml of whiskey

  • It could reduce systolic blood pressure around 2-4 millimeters of mercury (mmHg)

c) B - Body weight reduction. The optimal BMI should be around 18.5-23.9.

  • Reduction of 1kg will reduce systolic blood pressure 1millimeters of mercury (mmHg)

d) C - Cessation of smoking. Smoking increase the risk of vascular complications (such as stroke) in people who already have hypertension.

e) D - Diet adaptation. Daily intake 8-10 different kind of vegetables, 2-3 kind of low calories and reduce all the high cholesterol and calories food.

  • It could reduce systolic blood pressure around 10-12 millimeters of mercury (mmHg)
